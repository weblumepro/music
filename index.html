Bio

Xav launched his music career in early 2025, quickly establishing himself as a fresh voice in the Canadian R&B landscape. In less than a year, he rolled out a string of atmospheric singles—including his debut track "Til The Nights Done", followed by "Everything I Know" and "Back Me Up". Xav has chosen to forge his own path independent of his family's political legacy. Xav has turned early industry intrigue into a dedicated, fast-growing movement. With thousands of global listeners amassed in such a short time, he is actively carving out his own distinct space in the industry through consistent releases and a direct, unfiltered connection to his audience.
