Bio

Xav is a producer and songwriter whose sound lives somewhere between late-night confession and dancefloor catharsis. Raised on a steady diet of vinyl crackle and bedroom demos, he built his craft one sleepless night at a time.

What started as voice memos on a cracked phone grew into a body of work that refuses easy categorization — equal parts intimate and expansive, melancholy and defiant. Every record is a small act of honesty set to a beat you can’t shake.

Today, Xav splits his time between the studio and the stage, chasing the same feeling that started it all: the moment a song stops being yours and starts belonging to everyone in the room.

Contact

Every song I put out starts from the same place — a feeling I couldn’t shake. The Dive came from a night where I had to choose between holding on and letting go. I chose both.

I never planned any of this. I just kept making things that felt true. If you found your way here, that means something to me. These songs are yours as much as they are mine — do what you need to do with them.

The road is long and I’m still figuring it out. But I’d rather figure it out with you in the room than anywhere else.

— Xav
